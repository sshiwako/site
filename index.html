About Our Firm

Building Trust Through Professional Excellence

S. Shiwakoti and Associates is a distinguished Chartered Accountancy firm based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Licensed by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nepal (ICAN), we are committed to delivering exceptional financial and business advisory services that help our clients achieve their goals.

Our team of qualified professionals brings together extensive experience in auditing, taxation, corporate advisory, and business consulting. We take pride in understanding the unique needs of each client and providing tailored solutions that drive sustainable growth.

Our Services

Comprehensive Financial & Business Solutions

We offer a full spectrum of professional services tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses across industries.

Statutory Audit

Independent examination of financial statements ensuring compliance with legal requirements and accounting standards as per Nepal's regulatory framework.

Internal Audit

Systematic evaluation of internal controls, risk management, and governance processes to enhance operational efficiency and safeguard assets.

Tax Consulting & Compliance

Expert guidance on income tax, VAT, TDS, and other tax matters ensuring optimal tax planning while maintaining full regulatory compliance.

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Comprehensive financial record maintenance, ledger management, and preparation of accurate financial statements following NFRS standards.

Merger & Acquisition Advisory

Strategic advisory services for business combinations including valuation, due diligence, deal structuring, and post-merger integration support.

Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)

End-to-end assistance for foreign investors including company registration, regulatory approvals, repatriation compliance, and ongoing advisory.

Business Process Outsourcing

Scalable outsourcing solutions for finance, accounting, and back-office operations allowing you to focus on core business activities.

Due Diligence

Thorough financial, legal, and operational investigation services for informed investment decisions and risk assessment in transactions.

Corporate Compliance

Ensuring adherence to Companies Act, Nepal Rastra Bank directives, and other regulatory requirements with timely filings and documentation.
